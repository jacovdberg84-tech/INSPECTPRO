# InspectPro Operator Quick Guide (1-Page)

## Purpose
Use this guide for fast, consistent daily capture in InspectPro so maintenance and dashboard data stay accurate in Ironlog.

## Start of Shift (2-3 minutes)
- Open: `http://localhost:3002/`
- Confirm your machine/asset is correct.
- Enter operator name and signature clearly.
- Check hour meter before you start work.

## Daily Inspection Flow
1. Complete inspection checklist carefully.
2. Enter notes for any defect, risk, or unusual condition.
3. Enter correct hour meter reading.
4. Submit once and wait for confirmation message.

## If You See a Warning Banner
- A warning can appear when another station/operator recently submitted on the same asset.
- This does **not** block your submission.
- Action:
  - Confirm you selected the right asset.
  - Confirm hour reading is current.
  - Continue with factual notes.

## Breakdown Reporting Flow
- Report breakdown immediately from operator page.
- Capture clear fault description.
- Add photos where available.
- Submit and wait for success message.

## Data Quality Rules (Very Important)
- Never guess hour readings.
- Never skip notes when there is a real issue.
- Keep naming consistent for operators/components.
- One clean submission is better than multiple repeated clicks.

## End of Shift Checklist
- Confirm your last submission succeeded.
- Re-check any major fault logged during shift.
- Inform supervisor/artisan team of urgent issues.

## Common Issues and Quick Fixes
- **Page looks old / missing changes:** press `Ctrl + F5`.
- **Submit feels stuck:** wait a few seconds; do not multi-click repeatedly.
- **Wrong asset selected:** correct it before submitting.

## Escalate Immediately If
- Critical safety defect is found.
- Machine cannot operate safely.
- Repeated faults on same component in same day.
